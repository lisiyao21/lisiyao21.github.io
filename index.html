Li Siyao

Siyao is currently a fourth-year PhD student (candidate) in MMLab of Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, advised by Prof. Chen Change Loy. Before that, he was a full-time researcher in SenseTime Research, working closely with Quan Wang, Wenxiu Sun and Chao Dong. His current interests include AI techniques for content creation.
